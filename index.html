NO2 and PM2.5 Levels Visualization

This visualization project seeks to understand NO2 and PM2.5 level distribution across differents parts of our world. Begin by selecting the "Regional View" button below to view average pollutant levels by region. Drill down further to see detailed data for the countries belonging to specific regions by clicking any of the bars in the Regional View chart. Then, click on any of the countries (bars) in the Countries by Region View chart to get the country specfic data.
